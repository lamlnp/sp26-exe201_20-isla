# IslaMind User Guide

IslaMind is a private self-reflection web app for checking in with your mood, writing journal entries, getting gentle AI reflections, using CBT-style thought records, and drawing reflection prompts.

IslaMind is not therapy, diagnosis, medical care, or emergency support. If you may be in danger or feel unable to stay safe, contact local emergency services or a trusted person now.

## Getting Started

1. Create an account or sign in.
2. Complete onboarding, or skip it if you want to start quickly.
3. Use the dashboard to open mood check-ins, journal, reflection cards, CBT tools, profile, pricing, and safety resources.

## Main Features

- **Dashboard:** see your latest mood average, recent journal entry, quick tools, and safety link.
- **Mood check-ins:** save a 1-10 mood score with emotions, tags, and an optional note. Use history to view trends, top emotions, edit check-ins, or delete old entries.
- **Journal:** write private entries, add mood/emotions/tags, search past entries, edit entries, or delete them.
- **AI reflection:** generate a short structured reflection from a saved journal entry. It gives an emotional summary, possible theme, reflective question, and small action. If the entry looks crisis-like, normal AI reflection pauses and the app shows safety guidance instead.
- **CBT Thought Record:** walk through a guided thought record: situation, emotion, automatic thought, evidence for/against, balanced thought, and mood after reframing.
- **Reflection Cards:** draw a static prompt when you want a starting point for journaling.
- **Profile:** view your plan and real progress counts for journals, mood check-ins, and streak.
- **Pricing:** Basic is the default. Companion upgrade is mock-only for the MVP.
- **Safety:** read what IslaMind can and cannot do, plus crisis resource information.

## Useful Ways To Use It

- **Daily check-in:** spend one minute saving your mood, then look for patterns over the week.
- **Before a stressful event:** write a short journal entry, generate an AI reflection, and use the small action as a grounding step.
- **After a difficult thought:** complete a CBT Thought Record to compare the thought with evidence and write a more balanced version.
- **When you do not know what to write:** draw a Reflection Card and use its prompt as your first sentence.
- **Before a demo or class presentation:** create a journal entry about what you are feeling, then use the reflection question to prepare calmly.

## Plans And Limits

- **Basic:** includes the main features and 7 AI requests per day. The quota resets at `00:00 Asia/Bangkok`.
- **Companion:** mock MVP plan with unlimited AI requests after the mock upgrade.

There is no real billing, payment processing, invoice, or subscription management.

## MVP And Demo Notes

Some parts of IslaMind are intentionally simplified because this version is a school MVP, not a production healthcare or billing product.

- **Email registration:** sign-up uses email and password, but email confirmation may be disabled for demo accounts. This keeps classroom/demo testing fast and avoids blocking users on inbox setup.
- **Mock upgrade:** the Companion upgrade does not charge money. The button simulates a successful payment and updates the account plan so the team can demo plan behavior without adding real billing, invoices, refunds, or webhooks.
- **AI reflection:** AI output is a gentle self-reflection aid. It is not advice, treatment, diagnosis, or crisis support. Crisis-like entries pause normal reflection and show a safety-focused response instead.
- **Reflection cards:** cards are static prompts. They are not tarot, fortune-telling, or AI-generated predictions; they are just writing starters.
- **Profile stats:** journal count, mood check-in count, and streak use your saved app data. They are meant as light progress signals, not clinical measurements.
- **Privacy model:** your entries are tied to your signed-in account. For the MVP, there is no export/import, admin dashboard, or sharing feature.

These choices keep the demo focused on the core wellness workflow: check in, write, reflect, and review patterns safely.

## Current Limits

- IslaMind is a self-reflection aid, not clinical support.
- CBT records are saved, but there is no dedicated CBT history screen yet.
- Reflection cards are static prompts, not AI-generated cards.
- Desktop currently uses the centered mobile app layout.
