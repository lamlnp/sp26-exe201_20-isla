# IslaMind User Guide

IslaMind is a private self-reflection web app for mood tracking, journaling, gentle AI reflection, CBT-style thought records, and reflection prompts.

It is not therapy, diagnosis, medical care, or emergency support. If you may be in danger or feel unable to stay safe, contact local emergency services or a trusted person now.

## Getting Started

1. Create an account or sign in.
2. Complete onboarding, or skip it.
3. Use the dashboard to open Mood, Journal, Reflection, CBT, Profile, Pricing, and Safety.

## What Works Now

- **Mood check-ins:** save mood score, emotions, tags, and notes; view history; edit or delete entries.
- **Journal:** create, edit, search, and delete private journal entries.
- **AI reflection:** generate a structured reflection from a saved journal entry. Live AI is disabled for now; fallback/demo mode is active. Crisis-like text shows a safety fallback.
- **CBT Thought Record:** complete and save a thought record with a mood-after score and note.
- **Reflection Card:** draw a static journaling prompt.
- **Profile and pricing:** view your plan and use the mock Companion upgrade.
- **Safety:** read what the app can and cannot do.

## Plans And Limits

- **Basic:** all main features, with 7 AI requests per day. The quota resets at `00:00 Asia/Bangkok`.
- **Companion:** mock MVP plan with unlimited AI requests after the mock upgrade.

There is no real billing or payment processing.
